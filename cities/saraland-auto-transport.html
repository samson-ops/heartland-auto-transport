Open Auto Transport from Saraland

Open transport is the most common and cost-effective option for Saraland customers shipping standard passenger vehicles. Enclosed Auto Transport from Saraland

For luxury vehicles, classic cars, exotics, antiques, or any high-value vehicle, enclosed transport provides protection inside a fully enclosed trailer. Frequently Asked Questions — Saraland Auto Transport

How much does it cost to ship a car from Saraland, Alabama?

Cost depends on destination and vehicle type. Short routes from Saraland run $350-$700, mid-distance moves $700-$1,300, and cross-country shipments $1,100-$1,800 on open transport. Enclosed transport runs 60-80% more. See the route pricing list above for specific Saraland destinations.

How long does auto transport from Saraland take?

Total time from booking to delivery typically runs 5-15 days for most shipments. Pickup happens 1-5 days after booking. Transit time depends on distance: 1-3 days for short routes (in-state, neighboring states), 3-5 days for mid-distance (Texas, Florida, Carolinas), and 7-10 days for cross-country shipments from Saraland to the West Coast or Pacific Northwest.

Do you offer door-to-door service in Saraland?

Yes. We pick up at your specific Saraland address and deliver to your specified destination address. For locations carriers can't physically reach (narrow streets, low-clearance parking, gated communities with size restrictions), we coordinate a meeting point at a nearby commercial location — typically within a mile of your address.

Is my vehicle insured during transport?

Yes. Every carrier in our network maintains active FMCSA-required cargo insurance, with coverage limits typically $100,000-$250,000 per vehicle for standard transport (higher coverage available for luxury and exotic vehicles in our enclosed transport network). Insurance covers damage that occurs during transit — pickup-to-delivery. Pre-existing damage documented on the Bill of Lading is not covered.

Can I ship a non-running vehicle from Saraland?

Yes. Inoperable vehicles require a carrier equipped with a winch and typically incur a $100-$200 surcharge. Let our dispatcher know at booking that your vehicle is non-running so we can assign the appropriate carrier. Common reasons Saraland customers ship non-running vehicles: project cars, recent purchases that need work, vehicles awaiting parts, and inheritance vehicles being moved between family members.

How far in advance should I book auto transport from Saraland?

Book 4-6 weeks in advance for the best pricing and reliable scheduling. Last-minute shipments (within 10 days of desired pickup) often cost 25-40% more because carriers know you have limited flexibility. Summer months (May-September) and holiday weeks (Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year) require even earlier booking due to high demand.

What should I do to prepare my vehicle for shipping?

Wash the vehicle thoroughly, take detailed photos of every angle, reduce fuel to a quarter tank, remove all personal items above the window line, disable any aftermarket alarm systems, and provide two sets of keys to the driver. See our preparation guide for the complete 10-step checklist.
