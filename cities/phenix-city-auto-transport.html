Open Auto Transport from Phenix City

Open transport is the most common and cost-effective option for Phenix City customers shipping standard passenger vehicles. Your car ships on a multi-deck open carrier — the same type that delivers new vehicles to Phenix City-area dealerships every day. Open transport is appropriate for daily-driver sedans, SUVs, pickups, family vehicles, and most used cars purchased online. It's the industry standard for over 90% of shipments and saves significantly compared to enclosed transport.

Enclosed Auto Transport from Phenix City

For luxury vehicles, classic cars, exotics, antiques, or any high-value vehicle, enclosed transport provides protection inside a fully enclosed trailer. Enclosed shipping shields your vehicle from weather, road debris, and the elements during transit. Phenix City customers with Mercedes, Porsche, Ferrari, restored classics, show cars, or vehicles with custom paint typically choose enclosed transport. Expect to pay 60-80% more than open transport, but the protection is worth it for vehicles in this category.

Frequently Asked Questions — Phenix City Auto Transport

How much does it cost to ship a car from Phenix City, Alabama?

Cost depends on destination and vehicle type. Short routes from Phenix City run $350-$700, mid-distance moves $700-$1,300, and cross-country shipments $1,100-$1,800 on open transport. Enclosed transport runs 60-80% more. See the route pricing list above for specific Phenix City destinations.

How long does auto transport from Phenix City take?

Total time from booking to delivery typically runs 5-15 days for most shipments. Pickup happens 1-5 days after booking. Transit time depends on distance: 1-3 days for short routes (in-state, neighboring states), 3-5 days for mid-distance (Texas, Florida, Carolinas), and 7-10 days for cross-country shipments from Phenix City to the West Coast or Pacific Northwest.

Do you offer door-to-door service in Phenix City?

Yes. We pick up at your specific Phenix City address and deliver to your specified destination address. For locations carriers can't physically reach (narrow streets, low-clearance parking, gated communities with size restrictions), we coordinate a meeting point at a nearby commercial location — typically within a mile of your address.

Is my vehicle insured during transport?

Yes. Every carrier in our network maintains active FMCSA-required cargo insurance, with coverage limits typically $100,000-$250,000 per vehicle for standard transport (higher coverage available for luxury and exotic vehicles in our enclosed transport network). Insurance covers damage that occurs during transit — pickup-to-delivery. Pre-existing damage documented on the Bill of Lading is not covered.

Can I ship a non-running vehicle from Phenix City?

Yes. Inoperable vehicles require a carrier equipped with a winch and typically incur a $100-$200 surcharge. Let our dispatcher know at booking that your vehicle is non-running so we can assign the appropriate carrier. Common reasons Phenix City customers ship non-running vehicles: project cars, recent purchases that need work, vehicles awaiting parts, and inheritance vehicles being moved between family members.

How far in advance should I book auto transport from Phenix City?

Book 4-6 weeks in advance for the best pricing and reliable scheduling. Last-minute shipments (within 10 days of desired pickup) often cost 25-40% more because carriers know you have limited flexibility. Summer months (May-September) and holiday weeks (Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year) require even earlier booking due to high demand.

What should I do to prepare my vehicle for shipping?

Wash the vehicle thoroughly, take detailed photos of every angle, reduce fuel to a quarter tank, remove all personal items above the window line, disable any aftermarket alarm systems, and provide two sets of keys to the driver. See our preparation guide for the complete 10-step checklist.
