Our Story

Heartland Auto Transport was founded in Alabama with a clear mission: to bring honesty, transparency, and Southern hospitality to an industry that too often does the opposite. Anyone who's ever tried to ship a vehicle knows the typical experience — lowball quotes that mysteriously increase at delivery, drivers you can't reach, dispatchers who pass you between departments, and pricing structures that seem designed to confuse rather than clarify.

We knew there was a better way. Auto transport isn't complicated when it's done right. Vehicles get picked up. They get delivered. Customers get treated with respect along the way. That's it. The complications come from companies that prioritize squeezing margin over building relationships — and we built Heartland to be the opposite kind of company.

From day one, we've operated on three principles: quote it accurately, deliver it safely, and answer the phone when our customers call. That's not a marketing slogan; it's how we actually run the business. The five-star reviews we've earned on Google and Yelp aren't bought or solicited — they come from customers who experienced the difference and wanted to say so.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Four principles that shape every decision we make, every quote we deliver, and every shipment we run.

Transparent Pricing

The quote you accept is the price you pay. No fuel surcharges added later. No "carrier rate increases" sprung on you mid-transit. No surprise fees revealed at delivery. Our quotes are all-in — and that's exactly what you pay. This single principle has cost us short-term margin and earned us long-term trust.

Vetted Carrier Partners

Every carrier we dispatch to is verified for active FMCSA authority, current cargo insurance with adequate coverage, a clean safety record, and verified customer feedback. We don't use cheap carriers to win lowball quotes — we use reliable carriers because that's what protects your vehicle and our reputation.

Real People, Real Support

When you call Heartland, you reach a real person. No phone trees that loop endlessly. No outsourced call centers. Our dispatchers are based in Alabama, work full-time on transport logistics, and stay with your shipment from quote to delivery. You'll know your dispatcher's name and direct line.

Honesty Over Optics

If your route is tough, we'll tell you. If your vehicle needs enclosed transport and you've requested open, we'll explain why. If the price is higher than competitors, we'll be clear about why — and we'd rather lose a quote on honest pricing than win one on a number we'll later have to renegotiate. Auto transport is full of misleading quotes — we won't be one of them.

Alabama-Based, Always

We're proud to call Alabama home. Our team knows every interstate in the state — I-65 north through Huntsville to Tennessee, I-85 east through Montgomery and Auburn into Georgia, I-20 east-west through Birmingham, I-10 along the Gulf Coast through Mobile, I-59 through the Northeast. We know the back roads, the rural pickup spots, the gated communities, the dealer lots, and the lake houses on Lake Martin and Smith Lake.

Being Alabama-based means more than a marketing tagline. It means when you call us, you reach someone who pronounces "Tuscaloosa" correctly, knows the difference between Mobile and Montgomery, and understands that an "Auburn" pickup might mean the university town or a smaller town near Opelika. Local knowledge translates directly into smoother shipments, more accurate quotes, and fewer surprises.
