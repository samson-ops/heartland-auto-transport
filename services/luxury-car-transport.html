Enclosed Transport for Luxury Vehicles

Almost every luxury car shipped through Heartland goes via enclosed transport. Why? Because luxury vehicles have features that benefit dramatically from full enclosure: lower ground clearance that's sensitive to road debris kicked up on open trailers, custom paint and finish work that costs thousands to repair, sophisticated sensor arrays for driver assistance that can be damaged by impacts, expensive wheels and tire combinations that are vulnerable to debris, soft-top convertibles that need protection from weather, and complex front grilles, splitters, and aerodynamic packages that are easily nicked.

Our enclosed luxury car carriers use hydraulic lift gates instead of ramps, eliminating the angle-of-approach issues that low-slung luxury sedans and sports cars face. They use soft tie-down straps that wrap around the vehicle's wheels rather than hard chains that contact suspension components. Many of our carriers offer climate control inside the trailer, important for vehicles with leather interiors and sensitive electronics being shipped through extreme temperatures.

Luxury Car Transport Cost from Alabama

Luxury car transport via enclosed trailer costs more than standard open transport — typically 60-80% more — because enclosed carriers are smaller (fitting 4-8 vehicles instead of 9-10) and provide significantly more protection. Realistic luxury car shipping prices from Alabama: Birmingham to Atlanta (Mercedes S-Class, enclosed) $700-$900, Mobile to Naples FL (Porsche 911, enclosed) $1,100-$1,400, Huntsville to Beverly Hills (Range Rover Autobiography, enclosed) $1,900-$2,400, Montgomery to Greenwich CT (Bentley Continental, enclosed) $1,800-$2,200, Tuscaloosa to Aspen CO (Lamborghini Urus, enclosed) $1,800-$2,300. Single-car enclosed transport is also available for the highest-end shipments, at a premium.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does luxury car transport cost?

Luxury enclosed transport from Alabama typically costs $700 to $2,400 depending on vehicle, distance, and route. Single-car enclosed transport (highest level of service) costs more. Cross-country luxury shipments average $1,800-$2,400.

Should I ship my luxury car open or enclosed?

Almost always enclosed. Luxury vehicles benefit dramatically from full enclosure — protection from road debris, weather, prying eyes, and the higher service standards of luxury-focused carriers. The 60-80% cost premium over open transport is well worth it for vehicles worth $80,000+.

Do luxury car carriers use soft straps?

Yes. Our enclosed luxury transport carriers use soft polyester straps that wrap around the wheels rather than hard chains contacting the suspension. This eliminates risk of damage to control arms, brake lines, and aero components.

Can you handle delivery to private storage or country club destinations?

Absolutely. We regularly deliver to private storage facilities, country clubs, private estates, and gated communities across the country. Tell us the destination's specific access requirements and we'll coordinate with property management.

Do you offer single-car enclosed transport for extremely valuable vehicles?

Yes. For vehicles worth $250,000+, ultra-rare exotics, or unique builds where any other car on the trailer is unacceptable, we arrange dedicated single-car enclosed transport. Expect to pay 2-3x standard enclosed pricing for this premium service.
