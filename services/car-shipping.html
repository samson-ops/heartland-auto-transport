How Car Shipping Works with Heartland

The car shipping process is simpler than most people expect. After you submit a free quote request, we match your shipment with one of our vetted carriers based on your route and timing preferences. Our drivers pick up your car directly from your home, apartment complex, business, or any specified Alabama address. There's no driving to a terminal, no waiting at a freight depot — we come to you.

Once loaded, your car is secured to a multi-car carrier and transported to your destination. You'll receive updates from your dedicated dispatcher throughout the journey. At delivery, you (or a designated representative) inspect the vehicle, sign the Bill of Lading confirming its condition matches pickup, and the keys are handed over. The entire experience is designed to be stress-free, transparent, and professional.

Open vs. Enclosed Car Shipping

Most cars ship on open carriers — the multi-deck trailers you see every day on Alabama interstates. This is the most affordable and common option, and it's exactly how new cars are delivered from factories to dealerships. For high-value vehicles like classics, exotics, or luxury models, we also offer enclosed car shipping inside a fully covered trailer. Read more about our open auto transport and enclosed auto transport services to decide which fits your vehicle.

Car Shipping Cost from Alabama

Car shipping costs depend on distance, vehicle size, route popularity, and timing. Here are realistic price ranges for common Alabama routes: Birmingham to Atlanta costs $400-$600, Birmingham to Dallas runs $700-$900, Mobile to New York City typically costs $1,100-$1,400, Huntsville to Los Angeles ranges $1,200-$1,600. Cross-country routes are longer in absolute dollars but cheaper per mile. We provide transparent all-in pricing with no fuel surcharges or hidden fees at delivery — the quote you accept is the price you pay.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does car shipping cost from Alabama?

Car shipping from Alabama typically costs $500 to $1,500 depending on distance and vehicle. Birmingham to Atlanta runs $400-$600, Mobile to New York costs $1,100-$1,400, Huntsville to Los Angeles ranges $1,200-$1,600. Get a free quote for accurate pricing on your specific route.

How long does it take to ship a car from Alabama?

In-state Alabama shipments complete in 1-3 days. Regional moves to neighboring Southern states take 2-5 days. Cross-country shipments from Alabama to the West Coast or Northeast take 7-10 days from pickup to delivery.

Can you ship my car from a Carvana or online purchase to Alabama?

Absolutely. We handle online car purchases all the time. Just provide the seller's address (Carvana hub, Vroom location, eBay seller, etc.) and your Alabama delivery address. We coordinate pickup directly with the seller — you don't need to be present at pickup.

Is my car insured during shipping?

Yes. Every carrier in our network maintains active cargo insurance covering your vehicle from pickup to delivery. We also recommend documenting your vehicle's condition with photos before pickup so any issues are easy to identify.

Do you ship inoperable cars?

Yes — we ship cars that don't run, but they require a carrier with a winch. Tell us upfront if your car is inoperable so we match you with the right carrier. There's typically a $100-$200 surcharge for non-running vehicles.

Can I pack items inside my car during shipping?

Most carriers allow up to 100 lbs of personal items in the trunk below the window line. Items are not covered by cargo insurance, so don't ship anything irreplaceable. Never ship firearms, hazardous materials, or perishables.
