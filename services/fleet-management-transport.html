What Heartland's Fleet Management Transport for Businesses Includes

Redeployment & Refresh

Fleet vehicles cycle on predictable schedules — typically 18-36 months for sales fleets and service vehicles. When vehicles roll off and replacements arrive, the old units often need to move to auction sites, remarketing centers, or other fleet locations while new units flow inbound. We coordinate these refresh cycles in bulk.

Multi-Location Logistics

Businesses with fleet vehicles in multiple cities need a transport partner who handles routes between any two points — Birmingham to Dallas, Atlanta to Phoenix, Boston to Tampa. We dispatch from any U.S. origin to any U.S. destination with consistent pricing and reliability across your entire network.

Account Management

Every fleet customer works with a dedicated account manager who knows your accounting codes, billing preferences, and scheduling patterns. No re-explaining the situation every time you call. Direct access, predictable execution.

Bulk Volume Pricing

Fleet pricing is fundamentally different from retail. Volume commitments, route consistency, and scheduling flexibility all translate to lower per-vehicle costs. Whether you ship 10 vehicles a month or 200, your pricing reflects the operational efficiency of the relationship.

Specific Services Offered

  • Bulk fleet moves — coordinated shipments of 5-50 vehicles between locations or to remarketing centers
  • End-of-lease return transport — return vehicles to leasing companies and remarketers on schedule
  • Fleet refresh coordination — synchronizing outbound old units with inbound new units to minimize coverage gaps
  • Service fleet relocations — moving entire service fleets when offices relocate or markets shift
  • Executive pool transport — handling pool vehicles assigned to traveling executives and senior staff

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can Heartland dispatch a fleet management transport shipment?

For most routes, we can dispatch a carrier within 1-3 business days of booking. Time-critical situations (hot shot, expedited) can dispatch same-day depending on origin and current carrier availability. Standard shipments dispatch on a 3-5 day pickup window with the carrier confirming a specific arrival 24 hours before pickup.

What does fleet management transport cost?

Pricing depends on distance, vehicle or load specifications, route popularity, time of year, and service urgency. Standard shipments range from a few hundred dollars for short routes to $1,500-$2,500+ for cross-country. Specialty services (enclosed, expedited, hot shot, oversize) cost more but the premium reflects real operational differences. Every Heartland quote is transparent and all-in — no fuel surcharges, no rate increases at delivery, no hidden fees.

Is my shipment insured?

Yes. Every carrier in our network maintains active FMCSA-required cargo insurance, with coverage limits typically $100,000-$250,000 per vehicle for standard auto transport. Specialty cargo (commercial freight, oversize loads, high-value vehicles) often requires supplemental coverage which we arrange and document at the quote stage. Insurance covers transit-related damage from pickup through delivery.
