Exotic Car Transport Is a Specialty, Not a Service

Shipping a Ferrari 488 or a Lamborghini Huracán isn't the same as shipping a Honda Civic. Exotic supercars have characteristics that make standard auto transport completely inappropriate: extremely low ground clearance (3-4 inches in many cases) that ramp-style carriers can't accommodate without damage, carbon fiber body panels and aerodynamic components that suffer from minor stone chips, six- and seven-figure values that need single-vehicle or limited-load enclosed transport, and specific operational quirks (electronic parking brakes, lift systems, transport modes) that only experienced exotic carriers know how to handle.

Heartland Auto Transport's exotic transport network is intentionally small and selective. We work only with carriers who specialize in exotic vehicle transport — typically those who serve major dealerships (Ferrari of Atlanta, Lamborghini Houston, McLaren Beverly Hills), high-end collectors, and exotic auctions like Barrett-Jackson, Mecum, and RM Sotheby's. These are not generalist auto haulers picking up a Ferrari as a side gig. They are the same carriers manufacturers use to deliver new exotics to dealerships.

How We Handle Exotic Transport Differently

Every exotic shipment with Heartland follows a specific protocol that standard transport doesn't include. We use hydraulic lift-gate enclosed trailers — never ramps. Lift gates raise the vehicle from ground level into the trailer with zero approach angle, eliminating any risk of front splitter or undercarriage damage. We use soft polyester straps over the wheels — never around the suspension, control arms, or chassis. Hard chains contacting suspension components is one of the most common causes of exotic transport damage. We coordinate vehicle access codes, lift system instructions, and transport mode procedures with the carrier in advance. Modern exotics like the Lamborghini Huracán or Ferrari SF90 have specific procedures for getting them onto a trailer that the driver needs to know before arriving. We provide single-vehicle or limited-load transport options. Standard enclosed trailers fit 4-8 vehicles. For seven-figure cars, we can arrange single-car dedicated transport — your vehicle is the only one in the trailer. We carry supplemental insurance options. Standard carrier cargo insurance ($250K-$500K) covers most exotics, but for hypercars and unique builds, we arrange additional coverage on request.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does exotic car transport cost?

Exotic car transport from Alabama typically costs $1,200 to $7,500 depending on vehicle, distance, and whether you choose multi-car or single-car dedicated enclosed transport. Hypercars and seven-figure vehicles usually warrant single-car transport at premium pricing.

Should my Ferrari ship in a multi-car or single-car enclosed trailer?

For Ferrari production models (488, F8, SF90, 812), multi-car enclosed transport is appropriate and significantly more affordable. For limited-production Ferraris (LaFerrari, F40, F50, 250 GTO), single-car transport is recommended due to value and uniqueness.

Can you transport an exotic with a lift system or air suspension?

Yes. Our exotic carriers know how to handle lift systems on Lamborghini, Ferrari, McLaren, and Porsche models. The carrier will coordinate with you to understand your vehicle's specific lift procedure before arrival.

Do you offer climate-controlled enclosed transport for exotics?

For certain shipments, yes. Climate-controlled enclosed trailers are available for vehicles with sensitive interiors, fresh paintwork, or factory-new condition where temperature extremes during transit could affect materials. There's typically a premium for this service.

Can you transport one-off custom builds, kit cars, or restomods?

Absolutely. We've shipped Singer 911s, Eagle E-Types, Ringbrothers builds, Petersen Museum restorations, and countless one-off customs. The key is upfront communication about the vehicle's unique characteristics so the carrier is properly prepared.

Can my exotic be picked up at a private residence or sealed garage?

Yes. Our exotic carriers regularly pick up from private residences, gated communities, country clubs, and sealed climate-controlled garages. We coordinate access and timing in advance.
