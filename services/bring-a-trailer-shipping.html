Bring a Trailer (BaT) has become the premier online auction platform for collector cars, modern classics, modified vehicles, and enthusiast-grade specialty cars. BaT vehicles range from $5,000 daily drivers to seven-figure concours-quality classics — and most BaT buyers want enclosed transport for protection. Heartland Auto Transport works with BaT buyers across the country, coordinating with private sellers, handling the unique logistics of collector-car shipping, and protecting valuable vehicles in transit.

What Heartland's Bring a Trailer Vehicle Shipping Includes

Enclosed Transport Standard

Most BaT vehicles ship enclosed by default. Whether it's a restored Porsche 911, a meticulously maintained Mercedes diesel, or a museum-quality muscle car, the 60-80% premium for enclosed protection is generally worth it for vehicles in the BaT category. We coordinate appropriate enclosed carriers.

Private Seller Coordination

BaT sellers are typically enthusiasts selling personal collector cars. They care deeply about the buyer experience and the vehicle's care during shipping. We approach these sellers with the professionalism and communication they expect — and our drivers handle the vehicles with appropriate care.

Documentation & Detail

BaT sellers and buyers often exchange detailed documentation — service records, original window stickers, history files, restoration photos. We coordinate transfer of these materials at pickup so they reach the buyer with the vehicle.

Show Car Logistics

Some BaT vehicles head straight from purchase to concours events, shows, or photoshoots. We coordinate timing-sensitive deliveries with appropriate carriers for show-car-quality protection.

  • Open transport for daily-driver BaT purchases where enclosed isn't needed

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can Heartland dispatch a bring a trailer vehicle shipping shipment?

For most routes, we can dispatch a carrier within 1-3 business days of booking. Time-critical situations (hot shot, expedited) can dispatch same-day depending on origin and current carrier availability. Standard shipments dispatch on a 3-5 day pickup window with the carrier confirming a specific arrival 24 hours before pickup.

What does bring a trailer vehicle shipping cost?

Pricing depends on distance, vehicle or load specifications, route popularity, time of year, and service urgency. Standard shipments range from a few hundred dollars for short routes to $1,500-$2,500+ for cross-country. Specialty services (enclosed, expedited, hot shot, oversize) cost more but the premium reflects real operational differences. Every Heartland quote is transparent and all-in — no fuel surcharges, no rate increases at delivery, no hidden fees.

Is my shipment insured?

Yes. Every carrier in our network maintains active FMCSA-required cargo insurance, with coverage limits typically $100,000-$250,000 per vehicle for standard auto transport. Specialty cargo (commercial freight, oversize loads, high-value vehicles) often requires supplemental coverage which we arrange and document at the quote stage. Insurance covers transit-related damage from pickup through delivery.
