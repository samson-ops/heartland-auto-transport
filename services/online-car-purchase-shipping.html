Online car buying has reshaped the used vehicle market. Buyers in Alabama can now purchase from any seller in the country: Carvana, Vroom, dealer websites, eBay Motors, Cars.com listings, AutoTrader sellers, Facebook Marketplace, Bring a Trailer, dealer auctions, and private parties. The catch: someone has to physically deliver the vehicle. Heartland Auto Transport coordinates pickup from any U.S. seller and delivers directly to your Alabama address — handling the logistics so you can focus on choosing the right vehicle, not arranging transportation.

What Heartland's Online Car Purchase Shipping Includes

Seller Coordination

Our dispatcher contacts the seller, confirms vehicle availability, arranges a pickup window, and handles the logistics. You don't need to mediate between seller and shipper. Whether the seller is a dealer, a Carvana hub, an eBay seller's home, or an auction yard, we handle the coordination.

Verification Before Shipping

Before dispatch, we confirm with you that payment is finalized, title transfer is clear, and the vehicle matches the listing. We don't pick up vehicles where these basics aren't confirmed — this protects you from common online buying issues.

Transit Visibility

Long-distance shipments from across the country can take 7-10 days. Throughout that time, your dispatcher provides updates, and you have direct phone contact for questions. No black box.

Delivery Inspection

When your vehicle arrives in Alabama, we walk through the standard inspection: compare condition against the pickup Bill of Lading, document any transit damage on the delivery BoL before signing, and verify accessories and items that were supposed to come with the vehicle.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can Heartland dispatch a online car purchase shipping shipment?

For most routes, we can dispatch a carrier within 1-3 business days of booking. Time-critical situations (hot shot, expedited) can dispatch same-day depending on origin and current carrier availability. Standard shipments dispatch on a 3-5 day pickup window with the carrier confirming a specific arrival 24 hours before pickup.

What does online car purchase shipping cost?

Pricing depends on distance, vehicle or load specifications, route popularity, time of year, and service urgency. Standard shipments range from a few hundred dollars for short routes to $1,500-$2,500+ for cross-country. Specialty services (enclosed, expedited, hot shot, oversize) cost more but the premium reflects real operational differences. Every Heartland quote is transparent and all-in — no fuel surcharges, no rate increases at delivery, no hidden fees.

Is my shipment insured?

Yes. Every carrier in our network maintains active FMCSA-required cargo insurance, with coverage limits typically $100,000-$250,000 per vehicle for standard auto transport. Specialty cargo (commercial freight, oversize loads, high-value vehicles) often requires supplemental coverage which we arrange and document at the quote stage. Insurance covers transit-related damage from pickup through delivery.
