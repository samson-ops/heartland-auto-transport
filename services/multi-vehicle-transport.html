Multi Vehicle Transport Services

Shipping 2 or more vehicles at the same time? Heartland combines multiple-vehicle moves onto efficient carriers with combined pricing that beats individual shipments. Perfect for families relocating, businesses moving fleet, and collectors transporting multiple vehicles.

Most auto transport is single-vehicle: one car, one customer, one delivery address. But many shipping situations involve multiple vehicles: a family with two or three cars relocating across the country, a business moving a fleet, a collector transporting multiple show cars, or a dealer shipping inventory between locations. Heartland Auto Transport coordinates multi-vehicle moves with combined pricing, simplified logistics, and the efficiency of putting multiple vehicles on appropriate single carriers.

What Heartland's Multi Vehicle Transport Includes

Family Multi-Vehicle Moves

Families relocating often have multiple vehicles — primary driver's car, spouse's car, teenager's car, sometimes RVs or boats. Shipping all simultaneously on coordinated carriers saves money compared to individual shipments and simplifies logistics on both ends of the move.

Business Fleet Coordination

Businesses shipping fleet vehicles — sales fleets, service trucks, executive pool cars — benefit from multi-vehicle pricing. A 5-vehicle move costs significantly less than 5 individual single-vehicle moves.

Collector & Enthusiast Moves

Vehicle collectors with 3-10 cars often need coordinated moves when relocating, attending shows, or distributing vehicles between properties. Multi-vehicle enclosed transport keeps the entire collection together and protected.

Combined Pickup & Delivery

When all vehicles share the same origin and destination, a single carrier handles the entire move. When pickup or delivery addresses differ, we coordinate split-stop logistics with the same carrier or coordinated carrier teams.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can Heartland dispatch a multi vehicle transport shipment?

For most routes, we can dispatch a carrier within 1-3 business days of booking. Time-critical situations (hot shot, expedited) can dispatch same-day depending on origin and current carrier availability. Standard shipments dispatch on a 3-5 day pickup window with the carrier confirming a specific arrival 24 hours before pickup.

What does multi vehicle transport cost?

Pricing depends on distance, vehicle or load specifications, route popularity, time of year, and service urgency. Standard shipments range from a few hundred dollars for short routes to $1,500-$2,500+ for cross-country. Specialty services (enclosed, expedited, hot shot, oversize) cost more but the premium reflects real operational differences. Every Heartland quote is transparent and all-in — no fuel surcharges, no rate increases at delivery, no hidden fees.

Is my shipment insured?

Yes. Every carrier in our network maintains active FMCSA-required cargo insurance, with coverage limits typically $100,000-$250,000 per vehicle for standard auto transport. Specialty cargo (commercial freight, oversize loads, high-value vehicles) often requires supplemental coverage which we arrange and document at the quote stage. Insurance covers transit-related damage from pickup through delivery.
