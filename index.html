Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to ship a car from Alabama?

Auto transport from Alabama typically ranges from $500 to $1,800 depending on the destination, vehicle type, transport method (open or enclosed), and time of year. In-state Alabama moves run lower; cross-country routes to California or the Northeast run higher. Use our quick quote tool for an accurate price on your specific route.

Do you ship cars from Alabama to other states?

Yes. We're based in Alabama and ship to all 50 states. We also handle inbound shipments — moving from anywhere in the country to any Alabama city, including Birmingham, Huntsville, Mobile, Montgomery, Tuscaloosa, Auburn, and every community in between.

How long does car shipping take?

In-state Alabama transport typically completes in 1–3 days. Routes to neighboring states (GA, TN, MS, FL) take 2–5 days. Cross-country shipments to or from Alabama generally take 7–10 days. Your dispatcher provides a realistic delivery window when you book — no overpromising.

Is my vehicle insured during transport?

Yes. Every carrier in our network carries full cargo insurance at no additional cost to you. Coverage certificates are provided before pickup and included in your transport agreement.

What's the difference between open and enclosed transport?

Open transport is the standard multi-car carrier you see on every interstate — affordable and reliable. Enclosed transport ships your vehicle inside a covered trailer, recommended for classic, exotic, or luxury vehicles needing extra protection from weather and road debris.

Do you offer door-to-door service?

Yes. We pick up at any Alabama address and deliver to any address nationwide — and vice versa. Our drivers get as close to your specified addresses as legally and safely possible, accounting for narrow streets or low-clearance areas.
